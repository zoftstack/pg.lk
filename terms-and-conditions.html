Refunds and Cancellations

Programmer.lk has implemented the following refund policy for fee-paying students studying in the Programmer.lk. Programmer.lk may issue refunds for fees paid by students only in exceptional circumstances that are beyond the control of both the student and Programmer.lk. Any requests for a refund will be evaluated solely based on the following criteria:

  • Category A: Failure by programmer.lk to offer the course as advertised and planed.

    A1. Programmer.lk has decided not to commence the scheduled batch.

    A2. Programmer.lk has decided postponed the commencement of the batch the student registered for, and the Student does not agree to wait.

    A3. Programmer.lk has significantly changed the schedule of the batch that the customer registered for, and the customer cannot attend classes.



  • Category B: Student reverses the decision to pursue the course.Refund claim under this clause are acceptable only if such request is made in writing, prior to the scheduled commencement of the course. Evidence will be required to support the claim.

    B1. Student is facing unforeseen financial constraints.

    B2. Student's work schedule is clashing with the class schedule.



  • Category C: Medical Reasons.

    C1. Medical conditions that prevent the student from continuing their studies, verified by a registered medical practitioner..

    C2. The death of the student or a close family member, substantiated by appropriate documentation.

    C3. Natural disasters or other unforeseeable events that impede the student's ability to attend classes, confirmed by an appropriate authority.


Important Notes:

  1. Refunds falling under category A can only be requested before or within 7 days of the start date. Any request made after this period will be deemed invalid.
  2. Refunds falling under category A will be granted to the requester in full, representing the entire payment made towards course enrollment, provided that the student has not availed of any learning sessions. Any payments associated with class attendance shall not be included in the refund computation.
  3. Refund requests under category B which are received AFTER commencement will be considered at the discretion of the Governing Board, and will not receive a full refund. Instead, a partial refund will be considered.
  4. Refund requests under category C will require evidence in the form of medical records / reports (confidentiality will be maintained)
  5. For refunds processed under category C, the refund amount will be determined by the Governing Board of Programmer.lk.
  6. For all categories, Fees paid to external awarding bodies (Pearson, University Partners, etc.) and taxes paid to local authorities cannot be refunded, as this is beyond the control of Programmer.lk.
  7. For all categories, if the payment was made through the Payment gateway, the gateway charges will not be refunded.

REFUND PROCEDURE


  1. A request for a refund of programme fees must be submitted in writing (please use the Refund Request form) by the student and / or the guardian wishing to obtain the refund.
  2. A refund will be granted according to the clauses in the refund policy of Programmer.lk. Any request that falls outside of this policy will be referred to the officer-in-charge of Student Affairs for consideration and the final decision will be taken by the Chief Operating Officer, as to whether it can be presented to the Governing Board of Programmer.lk for their final decision.

PAYMENT OF REFUNDS


  1. Refunds (if any) will be made within four to six weeks upon receiving a completed refund application.
  2. Refund payments will be made only in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR)
  3. Refund payments will be made by the payment gateway that you made the payment when you buy the course, and will be made out in the name of the registered student or the relevant guardian. Payments will not be released to third parties.
